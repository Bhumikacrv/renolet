False ceilings Shop in Bangalore

False ceilings, also known as drop ceilings, suspended ceilings, or secondary ceilings, are architectural elements that are installed below the main ceiling of a room. They serve both functional and aesthetic purposes in various types of buildings, including residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.
